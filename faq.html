FAQs

1. Why own an IBM Model M keyboard?

Many users enjoy the "clicky" feel and vintage look to this model,
and its reliable design has not significantly changed over time.

2. Do people still use Model M keyboards today?

Yes! Some people prefer an older model of keyboard, and the
Model M is still compatible with most computers today.

1. Are Model M keyboards still being produced?

No, production of the classic IBM Model M keyboard stopped in 1999.
However, you can still purchase one from Ebay or other secondhand sites.
