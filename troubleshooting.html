Troubleshooting

Issues with your IBM Model M Keyboard?

Try these common solutions:

Not Responding

Restart your computer & Check the connection and ensure it’s plugged into the right port.

Sticking Keys

Remove debris with compressed air or a soft brush.
Reseat the key cap if it feels loose or misaligned.

Inconsistent Clicks

Press down on loose key caps to ensure they’re seated properly.
Remove and reseat the key if the spring seems misaligned.

Still Experiencing Issues?

If your issue persists, visit the Contact
Information section for further support.

Missed Keystrokes

Avoid pressing too many keys at once, as older models have limits.
